Why E-Scroll

security

Protection Against Fraud

Unlike the traditional paper-based scrolls which can be forged, the e-Scroll has a unique signature and time stamped certificate which are captured in the system.

cloud_upload

Easy to Use

For online job applications, the graduates can upload or attach their e-Scrolls to be sent to their prospective employers or agents.

image_search

Employee Verification

The e-Scroll system also allows access by employers and agents to verify the qualifications of prospective employees.
